About Eligna

Since bathrooms are spaces that we use every day, their importance is far greater than we give it credit for. Bathroom is not just a place to wash off the grit. So, for your bathroom you are not just creating a practial bathing space; you are creating a place where you can get away and be yourself. Every aspect and component has to come together towards this goal while also meeting the practical needs of bathing. The bathrooms have changed. Beyond just function, it has evolved into a place of contemplation, revitalization, imagineering. At this point Eligna created with a great love for detail and no compromise on precision. Our products set new standards for the bathroom which truly satisfy the client's demand.
